## angles
- The frieze collapse diagnostic: identical scenes placed adjacent in a sequence become noise to the reader -> Your 460 posts likely have keyword-intent clusters where the crawler cannot resolve them into discrete units -> Use Search Console's query-to-page mapping to pull queries routing to multiple URLs; this reveals collapses directly without touching the CMS
- The fixed circumference: a cylinder seal's register space is immutable, forcing ruthless consolidation of redundant scenes -> Your one-week sign-off pipeline with one part-time writer is a fixed resource -> Solve the bandwidth problem by consolidating posts that cannibalize each other, not by trying to produce more
- The separator that earns its place: zone separators in seals only work when they truly differentiate adjacent scenes; decorative separators don't resolve collapse -> Your canonical tags and internal linking hierarchy are separators that must differentiate their targets -> Verify that canonical targets are themselves well-differentiated before routing clusters to them
