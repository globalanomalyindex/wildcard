## angles
- A forensic triage protocol sorts material before processing, not after. Classify the 460 posts on four signals (traffic trend, keyword overlap, recency, screenshot presence) into remediate/consolidate/freeze tiers; freeze posts that offer no measurable signal improvement, reducing effective work from 460 to ~230 without touching content.
- GSC query data reveals keyword cannibalization clusters; for each cluster, identify the dominant URL and consolidate thin posts into it or re-target them to non-overlapping intent. This changes the approval-queue math: cluster resolution counts as one approval, not multiple, and 30-50 overlapping clusters become throughput gains.
- When both posts in a cluster carry preserved link equity (200+ referring domains each), separate their impression footprints instead of consolidating — update one for navigational intent ("what is X") and the other for comparison/how-to intent ("X vs. Y") so GSC impression curves no longer overlap, converting competition into reinforcement.
