## angles
- Posts degrade non-uniformly (high surface area posts lose procedural content quickly, but retain intact conceptual explanation underneath) -> the corpus has preserved interior zones that don't require full rewrites -> identify which posts have salvageable concept-layer content and swap screenshots rather than rewriting entire posts
- Keyword cannibalization creates chimeric signal across multiple posts targeting the same query, causing fragmented authority and split traffic that doesn't resolve -> this is observable in Search Console without touching the posts -> consolidate signal onto the canonical post with strongest existing authority and redirect weaker posts into it
- Editorial approval slots are finite and irreplaceable (one week per piece, hard Q4 deadline) -> cutting from the wrong zone wastes the capacity -> run a non-destructive crawl and Search Console analysis first to identify which cannibalization clusters have high restoration cost, then prioritize sign-off slots for the clusters with strong lead posts
