# v1 diagnosis: why arm B (wildcard) scored lowest on genuineness

## root cause

Arm B's genuineness penalty is an EMIT-STAGE presentation failure, not a reach failure. The wildcard correctly hands the model a genuinely distant donor and the structure-mapping search correctly finds a real isomorphism; but the skill's accept-test lets a strand SHIP while the donor's structure is still expressed in the donor's own vocabulary and altitude, before it has been "spent" — cashed out into a concrete, executable move stated in the USER's domain. Concretely, the skill's terminus criteria are too permissive in exactly one place: the structure-mapping bar accepts a transfer of "a method, a failure mode, a constraint, OR a next question" (structure-mapping.md test #3 / connecting.md step 2), and the seed's third beat is defined only as "the provocation — what is worth exploring, and why it works in my world" (structure-mapping.md "The seed shape", SKILL.md step 5). Both permit a strand to terminate at a reframe, a relabel, or donor-flavored diagnosis ("this is a structural exit point, not a motivation problem"; "diagenetic vs structural barriers") with no donor-free imperative. The model's adjacent picks (arms A/C) get scored higher not because they are nearer but because nearer donors are easy to cash out, so they incidentally always land on a number/checklist/named action; arm B's distant donors require a deliberate translation step the skill never mandates, so the distance stays visible "as decoration" and the panel reads visible-but-uncashed analogy as ungenuine. In one line: the skill optimizes for FINDING the isomorphism but never forces SPENDING it, so its most distant (and most valuable) connections arrive un-discharged and get penalized for the surface they fail to shed.

## fix levers (ranked)

### [P1] Add a hard removability accept-test as the gating criterion before any strand ships: 'If I delete the source-domain sentence, does an executable instruction survive?' A strand ships only if its final clause is a donor-free imperative the plainest reading of the problem would NOT already produce. If the move cannot be stated without the donor's nouns, the mapping is not yet earned — drop the strand or dig one step deeper; do not ship it.
- where: structure-mapping.md — rewrite test #3 ('Does something transfer?') from the permissive 'a method, a failure mode, a constraint, a next question' to a removability test that REQUIRES a concrete donor-free move; mirror it as the emit gate in connecting.md step 2; reference it in SKILL.md step 4 ('offer only what genuinely maps') so the bar is unmistakably an executable-move bar, not a transfer-of-anything bar.
- why: This is the single highest-leverage change because all three lenses converge on it and it directly converts the diagnosed root cause into a binary test. It fixes the 'terminates in metaphor' failures (out-54 3.75, out-03 4.75) and the 'donor vocabulary survives into the offering' failures (out-61 4.00, out-67 4.50) at one stroke, and it cannot be satisfied by picking a nearer donor — a distant donor that IS cashed out passes it (out-48 6.50, out-74 6.25 already do), so it preserves genuine distance.

### [P2] Make the seed's third beat a mandatory three-part strand schema: structural-insight -> explicit mapping -> CONCRETE MOVE, where the third slot must contain an executable verb plus at least one falsifiable specific (a number, threshold, named artifact, checklist, or field). Explicitly reject a third slot that is a reframe, a relabel, or a 'treat X as Y' with no executable verb. Demote source-domain vocabulary to at most one gloss clause.
- where: structure-mapping.md 'The seed shape' (redefine beat 3 from 'provocation' to 'concrete move with a falsifiable specific') and SKILL.md step 5 (change 'three-beat seed shape (noticing -> mapping -> provocation)' to the three-part schema and add the 'demote donor vocabulary to one gloss clause' rule).
- why: The current seed shape (noticing -> mapping -> provocation, 'what is worth exploring and why it works in my world') actively licenses the diagnosis-only and abstract-restatement failures (out-34 3.75, out-55 5.00, out-12 5.75) because 'worth exploring' is satisfied by a reframe. Forcing the third beat to be an action with a falsifiable specific is what the top arm-C/B outputs already do (out-11's 'half the churn rate', out-74's literal ratio formula). Second priority because it operationalizes lever 1 into the output template the model actually fills.

### [P3] Add an explicit per-strand release / accept-test gate with a default cap of ~3 strands and a 'name what you dropped' habit: a strand ships only if it (a) yields a move the plainest reading would not already produce and (b) survives a one-line 'why is this not the previous strand reworded?' check. Reframe conviction from 'force a bridge from THIS donor' to 'assume A bridge exists; if only a surface rhyme is found, dig to the structural layer or RELEASE this donor — per-strand release is routine, only whole-task abstention is the rare earned terminus.'
- where: connecting.md step 3 (strengthen 'drop the rest without ceremony' into an explicit accept-test + redundancy collapse + 'name which you dropped') and SKILL.md 'Search from conviction; offer with rigor' paragraph (clarify conviction = 'A connection exists' not 'THIS donor connects'; make per-strand release routine vs the rare whole-task terminus).
- why: The discipline lens shows the low scorers over-offer: they fan out weak/redundant strands (out-61's four angles with one load-bearing, out-34's three facings of one move) and force the first surface rhyme (out-67's 'pressure differential' relabel of 'friction', out-88's interleave that vanishes on contact). The conviction posture in SKILL.md currently reads as 'this donor connects' rather than 'a connection exists', nudging the model to keep doomed strands. Making per-strand dropping cheap and explicitly routine — while keeping whole-task abstention rare — raises the average genuineness by removing the dilutive strands the panel punishes.

### [P4] Require each shipped angle to be saturated in the USER's concrete particulars: name at least two specifics from the user's own problem (specific parts, file types, alert names, exact timestamps) and cap source-domain description to a single sentence. Bias the prompt to 'spend the analogy in the first clause, spend the rest of the angle in the user's domain.'
- where: structure-mapping.md (add to 'The seed shape': anchor each beat in >=2 user particulars, cap donor description to one sentence) and specializing.md (add a closing rule that the practitioner's vocabulary appears as a one-clause gloss, never as the load-bearing noun phrase of the move).
- why: Lower-priority because levers 1-3 largely subsume it, but it independently fixes the 'source-domain framing crowds out the user's particulars' failures (out-14 4.50, out-88 4.75) where bullets are spent narrating donor physics while the user's world stays thin — contrasted with the high scorers anchored in 'zip codes and parts used', 'fuel gauge photo'. It also reinforces distance-preservation: the borrowed structure becomes scaffolding riding on concrete user detail rather than the subject matter.

## predicted effect

Raises arm B genuineness toward parity with arms A/C WITHOUT lifting fabrication and WITHOUT shrinking the draw distance. Mechanism: the removability accept-test (lever 1) and the executable-move third beat (lever 2) convert the existing presentation cost into a forced translation step, so the same distant donors that now score 3.75-4.75 (out-54, out-34, out-61, out-67, out-03) get pushed toward where arm B's own best already sit (out-48 6.50, out-74 6.25) — the ceiling shows the floor is reachable on identical distance. Honest bounds: (1) it will NOT make arm B exceed A/C, only approach parity, because some distance genuinely costs a clause of glossing the panel may still mildly discount; expect the B mean to close most, not all, of the gap. (2) Strand count per offering will drop (lever 3 caps and collapses redundant strands), which is intended — scoring should reward distinct executable moves over paragraph count; a 2-strand offering with two real moves should now outrank a 4-strand one with one. (3) Fabrication should stay flat or fall, not rise: every lever tightens the accept-test (ship LESS, more discharged), and the removability test is strictly harder to pass than the current bar, so it cannot manufacture connections — a strand that can't be cashed out is dropped, landing in honest per-strand release rather than a forced ship. The one risk to watch on re-test: over-correction toward terse checklists that strip the donor entirely (losing the 'why it works' beat); the gloss-clause allowance in levers 2 and 4 is calibrated to keep one sentence of donor structure visible so the connection still reads as a genuine cross-domain insight, not a bare to-do.

### lens: discipline
