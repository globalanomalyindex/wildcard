# wildcard v2 skill fix: seeding, not forcing

## philosophy

The wildcard is a seed, not a subject. The draw exists to steer the model's thinking somewhere its on-task chain would never wander: conditioning on a genuinely remote token injects associations, techniques, and directions the plain reading of the problem cannot reach. The payoff can take any shape - a direct structural rhyme, a real technique the donor field calls to mind, a hinted direction, a skeleton a real idea hangs on - and the output is judged by exactly one thing: is the resulting idea genuinely better, novel, useful, or interesting in the user's world? Never by whether it maps faithfully back to the wildcard. v1 made structure-mapping the gate every strand had to pass, and that cage caused the failure the blind study measured: it pressured the model to manufacture donor-jargon isomorphisms and ship them undischarged, ending in metaphor instead of a move. v2 demotes structure-mapping from cage to servant - it stays the sharpest mode by which a seed pays off and the standing check against pure decoration - and installs the removability test as the real gate: delete the sentence naming the wildcard, and an executable move in the user's own words must survive, one the plainest reading would not already produce.

Seeding-not-forcing tightens honesty rather than loosening it. Nothing is fabricated: no invented facts, mechanisms, or connections, and the wildcard is presented as inspiration ("drawing on X...", "X brings to mind...") rather than a proven structural law. Nothing decorative ships: every strand ends in a concrete move carrying a falsifiable specific, saturated in the user's particulars, with donor vocabulary capped at a one-clause gloss. Releasing a strand that only surface-rhymes becomes routine editing, because conviction means a good direction exists in the draw, not that this donor must connect; whole-task abstention remains the rare, honorable terminus of a wholehearted search that found nothing good. The draw stays exactly as distant as before. The fix forces the distance to be spent, not displayed.
