## angles
- Interleave pattern in tape remastering prevents data loss from physical damage by distributing bits across many logical blocks -> van parts mixes lack designed distribution, resulting in high second-visit rates -> implement a minimum van kit (top 8-10 parts) stocked on every truck before specialty inventory
- Physical form of a degraded tape determines preparation needs and must be characterized before playback begins -> dispatch runs on time-of-arrival with job form invisible when routing decisions are made -> use a short intake protocol (3-4 questions capturing symptom, equipment age, service history) to characterize parts needs before dispatch
- Tape binder failures cluster predictably at structurally stressed regions (edges, splices, reel ends) rather than randomly -> HVAC capacitor failures cluster seasonally and predictably in summer heat -> implement seasonal pre-stock protocol: before summer, audit last two summers' second-visit parts and double-stock the top five on every van
