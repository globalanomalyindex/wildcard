## angles
- Chemical systems at equilibrium run forward and reverse reactions at matched rates (appearance of stillness is rate-matching, not flux absence) -> excavators disappearing into aggregate utilization numbers without surfacing which ones are stuck -> measure (days since last completed rental) / (median turn cycle for that asset class) per asset; a ratio of 10 when it should be near 1 signals deviation from equilibrium, per-asset, continuous, without requiring manual inspection
- Thermodynamically favorable reactions can be kinetically frozen behind high activation barriers; catalysts provide alternative pathways with lower activation energy, moving the same material through faster -> inspection throughput piling up with clear favorable endpoint (machine verified and available) but only two mechanics -> lower the cold-start activation energy by having customers complete standardized condition reports at drop-off (photos, fuel level, visible damage against fixed checklist); mechanic no longer establishes baseline from scratch and the same two mechanics move more units per shift
- Buffers resist pH swings by pre-positioning reserve capacity already in equilibrium, not by fighting incoming acid reactively -> the Saturday 7-9am surge is a fully predictable disturbance hitting a system that may already have 12 units pending inspection -> clear the inspection queue Friday afternoon so Saturday's returns hit a system at equilibrium; pre-calculate how many units two mechanics can clear per Saturday morning, set Friday-afternoon targets accordingly, and decide whether to pull a mechanic off other work Friday afternoons during peak rental season
