## angles
- The opening 45 seconds is natural accumulated coating that forms before quality material underneath -> viewers encountering the setup layer before the substance -> move the degumming point earlier in the sequence so quality-signal is the first thing that touches them
- Thumbnails, titles, and topics operate at genuinely different stages of the funnel but are confounded in the outcome metric -> map the causal chain before testing (topic surfaces, thumbnail stops scrolling, title decides click) -> isolate which mechanism drives which to make hypotheses about which to hold constant and which to change
- The 30% retention figure is a single-point reading that hides the shape of the failure curve -> the granular shape inside the first 45 seconds is where diagnostic information lives, not just the scalar -> examine whether retention is decelerating smoothly or has a discontinuity at a specific point to locate the defect
- Once footage is shot and edited, the window for capturing irreplaceable moments is closed -> a shot-coverage checklist run at the end of each filming day (before cleanup) can pre-screen for internal structural breaks -> audit both cameras captured the irreplaceable joinery sequence before the garage is cleaned up
