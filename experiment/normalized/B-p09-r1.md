## angles
- Wear-scar monitoring detects damage after it's already accumulated; friction coefficient signals the mild-to-severe transition earlier -> p99 latency is a lagging indicator like scar volume; queue depth, IO wait, buffer pool pressure, and merge-tree stalls are leading indicators -> instrument leading indicators and alarm at threshold to gain lead time for load shedding or deferral before SLA violations.
- Multi-phase test scars become palimpsests with unattributed contributions from different regimes, making averaged metrics scientifically unusable -> p99 latency integrates contributions from SLA-tier customers, casual tenants, and batch jobs into one undecomposable number, preventing triage -> thread tenant ID uniformly through the request path as a prerequisite for meaningful rate-limiting and SLA enforcement.
