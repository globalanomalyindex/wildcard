## angles
- Edge-leakage occurs at seals under differential pressure, not through the membrane itself → credential management fails at edges (local dev environments) where friction is highest, not through the vault → reduce the pressure differential by relieving friction at the local-dev edge rather than only strengthening vault policy
- Masks exclude contents as legibly as they pass them if records are kept → inability to bound a credential breach stems from lack of an authoritative registry of what the mask was supposed to contain → establish a bidirectional mask audit (what passed, what was blocked) to make the exclusion zone legible
- Maintenance debt accumulates silently then fails catastrophically, forcing worst-case response → credential rotation schedules and ownership metadata are tools for knowing the mask's fouling state → a system with known fouling state (schedule + ownership) responds proportionately to events; unknown state forces total rotation
