## angles
- Artifacts separated from relational context are unnavigable; recipes are saved as screenshots but lose context (who ate it, when, what changed) -> like unprovenanced lifting where the object survives but its relational meaning dies -> capture context at the moment it's free, like a sentence while clearing the table, rather than trying to reconstruct later

- A site matrix works because relationships between artifacts are the evidence, not sorting the objects themselves -> recipes have a natural relational matrix (pantry overlap, dietary fit, household reception) but are stored as a disconnected pile -> map the relations instead of the formats: think "dishes that clear the current pantry" or "reliable weeknight meals where the gluten-free swap is tested"

- Recording at zero cost only happens in the present moment; not recording has unbounded cost -> recipe information is live only while cooking, then either costs real effort to recover or is lost -> develop a capture habit tuned to that window of irreversibility

- Fragile content eventually hits paywalls or rots; the window to copy the artifact's properties is while the original is still accessible -> links will eventually break, but recipe content (text, ingredients, technique) can be preserved -> treat "save a recipe" as copying the content, not bookmarking the URL
