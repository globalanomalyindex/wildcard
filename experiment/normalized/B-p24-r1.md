- Degraded samples still contain the signal; the extraction protocol needs to be designed to find it -> the hook signal exists somewhere in the 4-6 hours of material, but the editing workflow may default to chronological position as the only candidate -> revise the editing process to systematically extract high-value moments (visible problems, near-misses, tension points) from anywhere in the footage and surface them first, treating time-of-filming as irrelevant.
