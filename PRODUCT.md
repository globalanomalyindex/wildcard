# PRODUCT.md - wildcard

register: brand

## Product purpose
Landing page for "wildcard", an experimental Claude Code skill that draws a random
hyper-specific expert (real entropy, 344 niche disciplines) to seed non-linear creative
thinking in LLMs. The page is a portfolio piece: it must DEMONSTRATE the thesis, not just
describe it. The page itself is a seeded draw: a card deck dealt onto a measured
golden-ratio grid, different every visit, reproducible by seed.

## Users
Design-literate engineers, AI practitioners, and potential collaborators or employers
viewing a portfolio. Curious, unhurried, on laptops/desktops in daylight. They will
fact-check claims; nothing on the page may be simulated or faked.

## Brand and tone
Playful-but-rigorous. Lowercase, calm, honest. Nature-as-mechanism (pollination,
germination, outbreeding, stochastic resonance) without nature kitsch. Every "live"
element is real: real entropy, real session transcripts, seed parity with the actual
shell script, falsifiable from a terminal.

## Strategic principles
- Medium matches message, but only if real. No faked demos, no decorative claims.
- The modular grid IS the design language (measured from the user's own Chickpea
  generative system output). Modular yet coherent: many moving parts, one piece.
- Single viewport at desktop. Content lives in cells, never appended below the fold.
- Navigation IS reshuffling: changing view re-deals the page.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing pages (hero-metric template, identical card grids, gradient text).
- Em dashes anywhere in copy.
- Long scroll storytelling pages.
- Fake "AI magic" demos that cannot be reproduced.
